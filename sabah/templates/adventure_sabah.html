Adventure Sabah Package

5 Days / 4 Nights

Embark on an exciting journey through the heart of Sabah, where nature's wonders and thrilling activities await! This tour is perfect for adventure enthusiasts seeking to challenge themselves while exploring the breathtaking landscapes of Mount Kinabalu and the surrounding rainforests. Whether you're an experienced hiker or a nature lover, the Adventure Sabah Package offers a blend of adventure, scenic beauty, and local culture that will leave you with lasting memories.

Day 1: Arrival and Welcome

Begin your adventure in Kota Kinabalu, where you will be greeted by our friendly tour guide. After check-in at your hotel, enjoy a welcome dinner featuring local delicacies, followed by an orientation on the exciting days ahead.

Day 2: Mount Kinabalu Ascent

Wake up early for a once-in-a-lifetime experience—trekking up Mount Kinabalu, the highest peak in Southeast Asia. Accompanied by an experienced guide, you will traverse lush rainforests, discover diverse flora and fauna, and be awed by spectacular mountain views. The challenging yet rewarding trek is a highlight for any adventurer.

Day 3: Canopy Walk and Nature Exploration

After descending from Mount Kinabalu, you'll visit the nearby Poring Hot Springs to unwind and relax in natural hot spring pools. In the afternoon, you'll embark on a canopy walk that takes you through the treetops of the rainforest, offering a unique perspective on the ecosystem below.

Day 4: White Water Rafting Adventure

Gear up for an adrenaline-pumping white water rafting experience on the Padas River, one of Sabah’s most exhilarating rivers. Surrounded by towering mountains and dense forests, the rapids will keep you on the edge of your seat as you navigate through the twists and turns.

Day 5: Cultural Experience and Departure

Wrap up your adventure by immersing yourself in the local culture with a visit to a traditional village. You'll have the chance to learn about the customs and daily life of indigenous communities, followed by a farewell lunch. In the afternoon, you'll be transferred back to the airport for your flight home.

Included Activities:

  • Mount Kinabalu trekking
  • Poring Hot Springs and Canopy Walk
  • White water rafting on Padas River
  • Cultural village visit
  • Nature photography opportunities

Package Inclusions:

  • Accommodation (4 nights)
  • All meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner)
  • Guided activities and entrance fees
  • Transport within Sabah (including airport transfers)
  • Professional English-speaking guide

Price: RM 800 / person
