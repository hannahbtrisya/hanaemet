Eco Tourism Package

4 Days / 3 Nights

Discover the beauty of Sabah’s natural landscapes while promoting sustainable tourism with our Eco Tourism Package! Engage in activities that support conservation efforts and experience the rich biodiversity that Sabah has to offer. Perfect for eco-conscious travelers and nature lovers!

Day 1: Arrival and Orientation

Welcome to Kota Kinabalu! After your arrival, you will be greeted by our guide who will provide an orientation about the eco-friendly initiatives in the area. Check in at your eco-lodge and enjoy a relaxing evening surrounded by nature.

Day 2: Wildlife Sanctuary Visit

Embark on a guided tour to the Lok Kawi Wildlife Park, where you will learn about Sabah’s native wildlife and conservation efforts. Participate in activities that help preserve natural habitats and support local wildlife.

Day 3: Nature Trekking and Community Engagement

Join a guided trek through the lush rainforests of Kinabalu Park. Engage with local communities to understand their sustainable practices and partake in a community-based conservation project that contributes to the preservation of their environment.

Day 4: Cultural Experience and Departure

Before departing, enjoy a cultural experience with local indigenous groups, learning about their traditions and conservation methods. Afterward, you will be transferred back to the airport for your journey home.

Included Activities:

  • Visit to Lok Kawi Wildlife Park
  • Guided nature trekking
  • Community engagement activities
  • Cultural experience with local communities

Package Inclusions:

  • Accommodation (3 nights in eco-lodge)
  • All meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner)
  • Guided activities and entrance fees
  • Transport within Sabah (including airport transfers)
  • Professional English-speaking guide

Price: RM 900 / person
