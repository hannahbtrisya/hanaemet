Nature Retreat Package

3 Days / 2 Nights

Escape to the serene landscapes of Sabah with our Nature Retreat Package! This getaway is designed for those looking to reconnect with nature and unwind in the tranquility of the great outdoors. Experience the lush rainforests, stunning waterfalls, and peaceful surroundings while participating in rejuvenating activities.

Day 1: Arrival and Relaxation

Arrive in Kota Kinabalu and be transferred to your eco-friendly accommodation nestled in the heart of nature. Spend the day relaxing and exploring the beautiful surroundings. Enjoy a welcome dinner featuring local cuisine.

Day 2: Nature Walk and Waterfall Exploration

Join a guided nature walk through the lush rainforest, where you will learn about the diverse flora and fauna. After the walk, take a refreshing dip in the nearby waterfall and enjoy a picnic lunch surrounded by nature.

Day 3: Morning Yoga and Departure

Start your day with a rejuvenating yoga session in a tranquil setting. After breakfast, you will have some free time to relax before checking out and being transferred back to the airport for your departure.

Included Activities:

  • Guided nature walk
  • Waterfall exploration
  • Morning yoga session
  • Eco-friendly accommodation

Package Inclusions:

  • Accommodation (2 nights)
  • All meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner)
  • Guided activities and entrance fees
  • Transport within Sabah (including airport transfers)
  • Professional English-speaking guide

Price: RM 600 / person
